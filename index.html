PAOLO

BARRAD



Paolo Bersabe Barrado, born on June 6, 2003, is a 21-year-old student currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering at the University of the East Manila. A graduate with honors of the Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) program at the same university in 2022, Paolo has demonstrated a strong foundation in his field.

In addition to his academic pursuits, Paolo holds the position of Vice President for Technical Operations for the Society of Computer Engineering Students (SCPES) and Vice President (Internal) for the Automation, Robotics, and Mechatronics Society, both at the University of the East Manila. His leadership roles within these organizations highlight his commitment to both technical excellence and community involvement.

Outside of school, Paolo enjoys playing bass guitar for the artist Shane G , performing at gigs and concerts as her bassist. This hobby allows him to express his creativity and passion for music, showcasing his diverse talents and interests.
